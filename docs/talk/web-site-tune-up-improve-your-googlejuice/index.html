Web Site Tune-Up - Improve Your Googlejuice

tutorial

Description

We all have web sites, right? And in most cases, we'd like more people to visit our web sites. And, to a first approximation, that means we want our sites to rank higher in Google.

There's a dull side to SEO. All that stuff about keyword tracking and content marketing. That's not the sort of stuff that appeals to geeks. But there's another side that make Google more interested in your site. And that's what we'll cover in this class.

I'll talk through a dozen or so simple(ish) tweaks that you can make to your web site more attractive to Google. Anyone can make these changes. But most people don't (which is why they work).
